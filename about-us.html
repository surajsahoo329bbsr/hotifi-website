What is Hotifi ?

At hotifi we aim to democratize the internet service providing industry by letting each hotifi user sell their mobile or broadband internet at a price they choose while also ensureing that every hotifi user who buys internet from these sellers also would not have to pay unfair price to have access to internet. We use a pay-as-you-use policy which ensures that the buyers will only pay for the internet that they acctually end up using.
