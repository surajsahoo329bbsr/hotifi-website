Privacy Policy

 

SECTION 1 - WHAT DO WE DO WITH YOUR INFORMATION?

 

When you use a service from our app, as part of the buying and selling process, we collect the personal information you give us such as your name and email address.

When you browse our app, we also automatically receive your computer’s internet protocol (IP) address or mac address in order to block unauthorized access to wifi for the seller’s wifi security.

This is done in order to prevent excess data usage by any buyer.

Email marketing (if applicable): We may send you emails about our app, new products and other updates unless you unsubscribe from this service.

 

SECTION 2 - CONSENT

 

How do you get my consent?

When you provide us with personal information to complete a transaction, verify your VPA, sell, buy or refund, we imply that you consent to our collecting it and using it for that specific reason only.

If we ask for your personal information for a secondary reason, like marketing, we will either ask you directly for your expressed consent or provide you with an opportunity to say no.

How do I withdraw my consent?

If after you opt-in, you change your mind, you may withdraw your consent for us to contact you, for the continued collection, use or disclosure of your information, at any time, by contacting us at hotifi.help@gmail.com.

 

SECTION 3 - DISCLOSURE

 

We may disclose your personal information if we are required by law to do so or if you violate our Terms of Service.

 

SECTION 4 - PAYMENT

 

We use Razorpay for processing payments. We/Razorpay do not store your card data on their servers. The data is encrypted through the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI-DSS) when processing payment. Your purchase transaction data is only used as long as is necessary to complete your purchase transaction. After that is complete, your purchase transaction information is not saved.

Our payment gateway adheres to the standards set by PCI-DSS as managed by the PCI Security Standards Council, which is a joint effort of brands like Visa, MasterCard, American Express and Discover.

PCI-DSS requirements help ensure the secure handling of credit card information by our store and its service providers.

For more insight, you may also want to read the terms and conditions of Razorpay on https://razorpay.com. 

 

For sellers, we also request and collect your VPA in order to transfer money to your account that you earned by selling the internet.
