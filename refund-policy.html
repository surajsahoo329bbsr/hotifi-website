Refund Policy

 

Refund claim time limit

 

Our policy lasts 7 days. If 7 days have gone by since your purchase, unfortunately, we can’t offer you a refund.

 

Refunds (if applicable)

 

There are certain situations where only full refunds are granted: (if applicable)

You (that is the buyer) did not use any of the data that you paid a price for and/or any other unavoidable circumstances such as the data you wished to buy and paid forgot used by another buyer connected to the same network thus prohibiting you from using any of the data.

 

Any other scenarios other than the above-mentioned use cases are eligible only for partial refunds(if applicable).

Partial refunds are calculated by the amount of data you used and the price the seller of that internet data set for the internet rounded to the nearest lower whole number. 

 

Every refund initiated by us takes about 2-10 days to get back into your bank account, depending on the method of payment you used.

 

The refund can only be made through the same method from which the initial payment was done.

 

 For example, if you paid for the service via UPI then the refund will be sent to the same UPI ID the payment was made and to the same bank account the initial payment was deducted from. 

 

 

Suspended Refunds

 

Our application must always run while you are using any of our services even if in the background to run all the processes as they are supposed to. In case you accidentally remove the application from running or the application crashes for any reason whatsoever while you were using any of the application’s services the automatic refund will not be initiated when you are finished using our service. In that scenario, you again need to login into the app and initiate a request for a refund(if there is any) within 7 days of completion of the service upon which the refund was earned. 

 

Once your request is received and inspected, if your request is approved, then your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your bank account through the original method of payment, within a certain number of days. Please keep checking your bank account every 2 to 3 days.

 

 

Late or missing refunds (if applicable)

 

If you haven’t received a refund yet, first check your bank account again.

Next, contact your bank. There is often some processing time before a refund is posted.

If you’ve done all of this and you still have not received your refund yet, please contact us at hotifi.help@gmail.com.
