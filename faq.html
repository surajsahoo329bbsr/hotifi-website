What is Hotifi?

Hotifi is an android application that enables its users to sell any excess internet or buy in case the user needs extra data at a very cheap cost with a pay-as-you-use model. 

If a user could not finish all the internet data allocated to them (be it mobile or broadband) by their respective ISPs they can start selling this excess internet through Hotifi by setting up mobile hotspots or straight from their router in case they chose to sell broadband data. 

Similarly, If a user does not have proper access to the internet or the access to the internet is expensive in their area, they can choose to buy the internet from any nearby Hotifi hotspots and pay only for the internet they used. 

How do I fix the allow permission for turning on Wi-Fi?

On android pie or below, you might face this issue. To fix this:

  • 1. Goto to application permission 
  • 2. Select the Change Wi-Fi connectivity permission 
  • 3. Select Always allow

 The issue will be resolved. 

How do I report any dispute?

You can file any complaint or dispute here: hotifi.help@gmail.com . Add a screenshot of the issue to help us better understand the problem.

Which platform Hotifi currently supports?

Hotifi currently supports selling and buying mobile data and/or broadband internet on any device that has Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or above. We will gradually extend our product support to the remaining platforms very soon.

I am selling internet

How do I sell my broadband internet or sell from the router?

  1. First, make sure you’re connected to your WiFi on your phone and open the Hotifi application.(This is very important since if you get disconnected during the time you’re selling internet, Hotifi will not be able to monitor the internet usage through your router)
  2. Select the "sell internet" option on the home screen. 
  3. Select a price at which you want to sell your internet.
  4. Select the amount of data you want to sell.
  5. Copy the Hotifi provided WiFi name(SSID) and password into the admin page. (The exact process depends on your router but you can try typing http://192.168.0.1/ in your browser after connecting your device to the WiFi, this address usually works)
  6. After you are done with the previous step press the “Start Session” button, you will see a session is started immediately.

How do I sell my mobile internet?

  1. Turn on your mobile data.
  2. Select the "sell internet" option on the home screen. 
  3. Select a price at which you want to sell your internet. 
  4. Select the amount of data you want to sell.
  5. Copy the Hotifi provided hotspot name( SSID) and password into your personal hotspot setting.
  6. After you are done with the previous step press the “Start Session” button, you will see a session is started immediately.

How do I get paid for selling internet?

Sign up and link your UPI ID/VPA. You will start receiving payment whenever a nearby Hotifi user connects to your internet.

How do I determine at what price to sell my mobile data?

Hotifi lets its seller pick up a selling price of their own choice, however choosing an unfair price to sell mobile data will let potential buyers buy internet from another seller who would be willing to sell the same amount of data at a lower price. So it is recommended choosing the Hotifi recommended price for best results. To protect buyers from unfair data prices we have a price ceiling of ₹21 per GB. No seller is allowed to sell data at a higher cost than this price.

How a buyer gets charged?

Hotifi operates on a pay-as-you-use model. Buyers will pay exactly for how much data they used. So for example, if you are selling internet at a price of ₹10 per GB, a buyer will pay ₹9 if he/she uses 830 MB of data and not the full amount of ₹10. (1 GB = 1024 MB)

When do I get my payout?

You will receive your payment after the amount you have earned after selling internet exceeds INR 20 or 20 days have passed from the first day you sold your internet, whichever is faster.

How do I calculate my payout?

You will receive an 80% payout from the listed internet price. For example, if you sold ₹10 for 1 GB you shall receive ₹8 as your payout if the buyer used up 1 GB of internet. This 20% deduction is the service fee a seller pays to Hotifi.

How do I check my earnings?

In your Hotifi application, go into the payments and select earnings to have a detailed look at your past earnings.

How secure is a Hotifi hotspot?

Hotifi enforces Wi-Fi standard-compliant authentication and encryption methods to secure its communication channel. In addition to that, we use our own algorithms to secure your hotspots from intrusion. 

I am buying internet

How do I buy internet data from Hotifi?

  1. 1. Sign up with your Google or Facebook or with any other valid email you have.
  2. 2. Link your UPI ID/VPA.
  3. 3. Select the "buy internet" option on the home screen. 
  4. 4. Select any nearby Hotifi networks of your choice.
  5. 5. Select how much data you want to buy. (Do not worry if you end up not using all the data you purchased for. Hotifi will refund for the data you did not use)
  6. 6. Pay and get connected.

How do I make payment?

You will be asked to make a payment whenever you choose to connect to a nearby Hotifi network of your choice. Then you can make payment through any wallet or your UPI ID.

When do I have to make payment?

You will be asked to make a payment when you connect to a nearby Hotifi network. After you finish your session (disconnect from the network) Hotifi will calculate how much data you used and you will get a refund for the internet you couldn't use. This is explained in detail in “How do I get charged?".

How do I get charged?

Unlike prepaid or postpaid data plans Hotifi charges its users on a pay-as-you-use model. You will only pay for the amount of data you used. So for example, if a network is selling ₹10 per GB and you used only 100 MB of data then you will be charged only ₹1. The rest of the amount i.e., ₹9 will be refunded to your bank account through the same method from which the initial payment was made.

How do I check my internet usage?

In your Hotifi application, select the History menu and then goto to usage to have a detailed look at your past internet usage.

I have made payment but my android device did not connect to the hotspot. What should I do next ?

If your payment has been completed successfully and your android device did not connect to the hotspot, then it might be due to the following reasons.

Your android device version is Pie or below - 

  1. The seller might have given incorrect credentials in the hotspot setup due to which your device doesn’t connect to it.
  2. The seller might have closed the session at the time of your purchase.
  3. Any other unexpected issues.

Your android device version is Q or above -

  1. Due to technical and security constraints by Google, connection in android Q or above devices takes usually more time than android pie devices to connect. In this case please be patient for 10-15 seconds, and check your wifi search list of your device, and tap on the hotspot you recently bought. You can also click on the reconnect button to reconnect.
  2. The seller might have given incorrect credentials in the hotspot setup due to which your device doesn’t connect to it.
  3. The seller might have closed the session at the time of your purchase.
  4. Any other unexpected issues.

But in any case if you did not connect to the hotspot you bought, you will get an assured full refund if you simply select the disconnect button in the application. For unexpected reason you are not able to use the internet at all and you have made full payment then you will get a full refund.  

I have withdrawn my earnings, but did not receive money. What should I do ?

It usually takes 2-3 business days to transfer money to the seller’s account. It is a time constraint we cannot avoid. We do not verify your VPA (Virtual Payment Address) that you save in order to receive payments. For this we might send you an error message in application and an email to check your VPA and try initiating a withdrawal again. We will make sure that you get your earnings on time.

Why am I doing a speed test to sell internet data ?

To ensure that every user who chooses to buy internet through Hotifi would get fast internet connectivity, we initiate a speed test right before a seller is about to sell their internet. Only after passing these speed tests the seller is allowed to sell their internet.

We have different thresholds to pass for both a broadband seller as well as a mobile internet seller.

Everytime a seller changes his/her location they would have to go through the speed test before selling to ensure that particular area has proper internet connectivity. 

And in case you cleared the cache memory then you would also have to go through the speed test once again.
